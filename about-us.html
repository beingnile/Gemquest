About Us

GemQuest: Find Hidden Gems is a service business based in Kenya. Our mission is to help youth discover and save hidden gems, such as restaurants, sports facilities, and parks, on social media platforms. We aim to provide a platform that consolidates these saved locations into one place, making it easy for users to keep track of their experiences and share them with others.

Our vision is to create a community of explorers who are passionate about discovering unique and exciting places. We want to inspire young people to step out of their comfort zones and explore the hidden gems that Kenya has to offer.

At GemQuest, our goal is to provide a user-friendly website that offers various features to enhance the experience of our users. These features include a Maps integration, allowing users to easily locate and navigate to their saved gems. We also integrate with Uber and social media apps, enabling users to seamlessly plan their visits and share their experiences with friends and followers.

In addition, we offer a Bucket List feature that allows users to create a list of places they want to visit and track their progress. Our platform also provides information on amenities available at each location, such as parking, Wi-Fi, and accessibility options.

To ensure the reliability of our recommendations, we incorporate social features, such as user reviews and star ratings. This allows users to make informed decisions when selecting which gems to explore. We also provide estimated budgets for each location, helping users plan their visits accordingly.

Lastly, we offer a convenient sign-up feature that allows users to create personalized accounts, enabling them to save their favorite gems, interact with other users, and receive personalized recommendations based on their preferences. Our AirBnBs side navigation panel provides easy access to accommodation options near each gem, making it convenient for users to plan their trips.

At GemQuest: Find Hidden Gems, we are dedicated to helping young people uncover the hidden treasures of Kenya and create lasting memories.
